Frequently asked questions

Is PadhKhel free?

Yes � 100% free. Every activity, class and exam test is available to everyone with no in-app purchases or subscriptions. The app is supported by a small, child-safe banner ad.

Which classes and exams does PadhKhel cover?

School content from Play Group to Class 12, and exam prep for SSC, Railways, Banking, Teaching (CTET), Defence (NDA), UPSC, State PSC, NEET, JEE Main, AIIMS, CAT, CLAT, GATE, MHT-CET and CA CPT.

Does PadhKhel work offline?

Yes. All activities and your progress work fully offline. No internet connection is needed to learn.

Does PadhKhel collect personal data?

The app itself collects no personal data — your profile and progress stay on your device. It shows one child-safe banner ad via Google AdMob; for young learners ads are always non-personalized.

Does PadhKhel include NCERT books?

PadhKhel provides original, syllabus-aligned practice and links to the official NCERT website for textbooks. It does not host or copy NCERT books.
