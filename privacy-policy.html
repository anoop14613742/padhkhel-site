Privacy Policy

App: PadhKhel  ·  Last updated: 21 July 2026

PadhKhel ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy, including the privacy of children. This policy explains what information the PadhKhel Android app does and does not handle.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information ourselves. PadhKhel does not ask for your email, phone number, location, contacts, or photos, and has no server accounts. The optional profile (name, avatar, class) is stored only on your device.

Advertising

PadhKhel is free and supported by advertising served by Google AdMob. AdMob may process device information (such as advertising identifiers) to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's protection: for profiles in Class 8 and below, ad requests are flagged as child-directed and configured for non-personalized ads with a maximum content rating of "G", in line with COPPA and the Google Play Families policy. Personalized advertising is never used for child profiles.

Data stored on your device

Your learning progress (stars earned and which activities you have unlocked) is stored only on your device using local storage. It never leaves your device and is not sent to us or any third party. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Children's privacy

PadhKhel is suitable for children and families. Because we collect no personal data, display no ads, and use no tracking, the app is designed to comply with children's privacy requirements including the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU GDPR. Any purchase or settings action is placed behind a parental gate.

Purchases

PadhKhel has no in-app purchases and no subscriptions. All content is free for everyone. We never ask for or handle any payment information.

Links to other services

The app includes a link that opens the official NCERT website in your browser for textbooks. We do not host NCERT content. Once you leave the app, the privacy policy of the destination website applies.

Permissions

PadhKhel requests only the permissions it needs to function. It does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage of personal files.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the new version at this URL.

Contact
